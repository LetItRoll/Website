Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I book a lesson?
    A: The best way is to contact me is via WhatsApp so we can arrange a time that suits you and I can answer any more questions you might have.
  • Q: What is the price for a lesson?
    A: See the pricing section for both single lessons, bundle deals and test day package costing.
  • Q: What should I bring to my first lesson?
    A: Just bring your provisional licence, wear comfortable clothing and shoes with a good grip, and bring a notepad if you’d like to jot down notes.
  • Q: How long is a typical first lesson?
    A: Lessons are normally one hour long, but we can do two-hour sessions if you prefer longer practice.
  • Q: Will you pick me up/drop me off at home?
    A: Yes—if your address is within my teaching area, I’ll collect and return you at no extra charge. Just let me know when booking.
  • Q: Can I have a friend or family member come along?
    A: Sorry, only the learner and instructor are allowed in the car during lessons for safety and insurance reasons.
  • Q: What happens if it rains or the weather is bad?
    A: We drive in all reasonable weather. If conditions become unsafe (e.g. heavy snow or ice), we’ll reschedule at no extra cost.
  • Q: How soon can I start after I book?
    A: I usually have slots available within the next two weeks—just let me know your availability and I’ll always try to fit you in.
