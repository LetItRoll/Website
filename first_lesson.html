What to expect on my first lesson?

  • Introduction: We'll start by completing any remaining paperwork and introducing ourselves. I'll explain the lesson plan and answer any questions you have.

  • Vehicle Familiarisation: Get comfortable in the car. Adjust seat and mirrors, learn dashboard controls (indicators, wipers, lights) and how the pedals feel.

  • Safety Briefing: We'll go over basic safety checks: seat belt, head restraint, seat position, and a quick look at blind spots.

  • Engine Start & Controls: I'll demonstrate how to start the engine, move off, stop and use the handbrake smoothly.
  • Basic Manoeuvres: Practice gentle acceleration, braking, and steering on a quiet road or empty car park to build confidence.

  • On-Road Driving: When you're ready, we'll move onto local roads. Expect slow-paced practice on residential streets, focusing on turning and lane discipline.

  • Feedback & Recap: We'll end the lesson by discussing what went well and areas to practice before your next lesson. I'll give you tips and gentle homework (like mirror checks or pedal timing).

By the end of this session, you will hopefully feel a little more confident in handling the car and understand the basics. It probably won't feel like it but you'll be well on your way to being a driver!
