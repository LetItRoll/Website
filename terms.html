Terms & Conditions

1. Booking & Payment

All lessons and test packages must be paid in full before the lesson date. Payment methods accepted include bank transfer, cash, and major payment apps.


2. Cancellations & Rescheduling

Please give at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. If less than 24 hours’ notice is provided, a cancellation fee of £40 will apply.

Test rescheduling fees of £15 apply when shifting DVSA test dates or searching for cancellations.


3. Lesson Conduct & Safety

Students must hold a valid UK provisional driving licence. Wearing suitable clothing and footwear is required. Only the learner and instructor are permitted in the vehicle during lessons for safety and insurance reasons.


4. Instructor Responsibilities

The instructor will provide professional, patient, and safe driving instruction. Lessons will be delivered in a well-maintained automatic vehicle.

In adverse weather or unsafe conditions, lessons may be rescheduled for safety.


5. Liability

While every effort is made to provide a safe learning environment, Let It Roll and its instructors are not liable for any loss, damage, or injury sustained during lessons except where caused by negligence.


6. DVSA Test Bookings

Practical test bookings are managed through the instructor’s business account to avoid double bookings and ensure smooth transitions.


7. Privacy

Personal data collected will only be used for lesson booking and communication purposes in line with UK GDPR regulations.


8. Changes to Terms

Let It Roll reserves the right to update these terms and conditions at any time. Notice of changes will be provided on the website.

If you have any questions about these terms, please contact us.
